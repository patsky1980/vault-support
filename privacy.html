Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters

Last updated: December 17, 2024

Our Commitment to Privacy

Vault is built with privacy as its core principle. We believe your conversations are yours alone, and we've designed our app to protect them.

Key Principle: We collect only what's necessary to make the app work, and we never sell your data to anyone.

Information We Collect

Phone Number

We collect your phone number to create your account and allow others to contact you. Your phone number is stored securely and is only visible to people you have conversations with.

Display Name

The name you choose to display to your contacts. You can change this at any time.

Messages

Your messages are temporarily stored to enable delivery. Once the message timer expires, the message is permanently deleted from our servers. We cannot recover expired messages.

Device Information

Basic device information may be collected for app functionality, such as push notification tokens to deliver messages.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not read or access the content of your messages
  • We do not store messages after they expire
  • We do not collect your contacts or address book
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not use advertising trackers
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Deliver messages to your contacts
  • Enable others to find you by phone number
  • Send you important notifications about the service
  • Improve app performance and fix issues

Message Expiration & Deletion

Vault is designed around disappearing messages:

  • All messages have an expiration timer set by the sender
  • Once expired, messages are permanently deleted from our servers
  • Deleted messages cannot be recovered by anyone, including us
  • You can also manually delete conversations at any time

Note: While we delete messages from our servers, we cannot prevent recipients from taking screenshots or photos of their screen.

Data Security

We take security seriously:

  • All data is transmitted over secure, encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Your app passcode is stored locally on your device only
  • We use industry-standard security practices
  • Our infrastructure is hosted on secure cloud services (Firebase/Google Cloud)

Third-Party Services

Vault uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication - For phone number verification
  • Firebase Cloud Firestore - For secure data storage
  • Apple Push Notification Service - For message notifications

These services have their own privacy policies and are compliant with industry standards.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access - Request a copy of your data
  • Delete - Delete your account and all associated data
  • Portability - Request your data in a portable format
  • Correction - Update or correct your information

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email below.

Children's Privacy

Vault is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy in the app or on our website. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us
