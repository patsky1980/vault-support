About Vault

Vault is a secure messaging app designed for private conversations. Every message has a timer - once it expires, the message is automatically deleted forever.

Your privacy is our priority. We don't store your messages after they expire, and your conversations stay between you and your contacts.

Features

  • Disappearing Messages - Set timers from 30 seconds to 24 hours
  • App Lock - Protect your app with a secure passcode
  • Auto-Lock - Automatically locks when you leave the app
  • Emergency Wipe - Data is erased after multiple failed unlock attempts
  • Phone-Based - Connect using phone numbers, no usernames needed

Frequently Asked Questions

How do disappearing messages work?

When you send a message, you choose how long it will last. For you, the timer starts when you send it. For the recipient, the timer starts when they open the conversation. Once the timer expires, the message is permanently deleted.

Can I recover deleted messages?

No. Once a message expires or is deleted, it cannot be recovered. This is by design to protect your privacy.

Why do I need to verify my phone number?

Phone verification ensures that only you can access your account and helps your contacts find you securely.

What happens if I forget my app passcode?

After 5 failed attempts, all local data is wiped for security. You'll need to verify your phone number again to use the app.

Is Vault free?

Yes, Vault is completely free to use.
